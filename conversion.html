⚠️ Critical Bottlenecks Identified

1. Impressions to Clicks (83.4% drop)
Meta descriptions and titles need optimization. Current CTR of 2.1% is far below industry standard of 5%+
2. Users to Returning Users (78.8% drop)
Implement retention strategies: email campaigns, in-app engagement, value demonstration
3. Users to Key Events (93.7% drop)
Conversion paths need simplification. Consider reducing friction in signup/purchase process
4. Organic Search Conversion (1.2% vs Direct 11.4%)
Landing pages don't match search intent. Need targeted landing pages for search traffic
